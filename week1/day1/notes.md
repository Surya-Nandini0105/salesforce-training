# 📘 Day 1 Learnings – Salesforce CRM Basics

## ✅ Topics Learned

Today I learned the basics of Salesforce and CRM.

### 🔹 What is CRM?
- CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.
- It helps companies manage customer information, sales, and communication.
- CRM improves customer relationships and business growth.

### 🔹 Introduction to Salesforce
- Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform.
- It is widely used by companies to manage customers and sales processes.
- Salesforce provides automation, reports, analytics, and customer support features.

### 🔹 Salesforce Objects
I learned about the three main Salesforce objects:

#### 1. Account
- Represents a company or organization.
- Stores company details like name, phone number, and address.

#### 2. Contact
- Represents a person related to an account.
- Stores details like email, phone number, and job role.

#### 3. Opportunity
- Represents a potential business deal or sales process.
- Tracks sales stages and expected revenue.
